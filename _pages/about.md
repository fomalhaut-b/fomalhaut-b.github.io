I am a first year PhD student in Computer Science at [University of Wisconsin-Madison](https://www.wisc.edu/), supervised by [Prof. Sandeep Silwal](https://sandeepsilwal.github.io/) and [Prof. Misha Khodak](https://mishakhodak.com/).

Previously, I completed my MMath (Thesis) in Computer Science at [University of Waterloo](https://uwaterloo.ca/) under the supervision of [Prof. Therese Biedl](https://cs.uwaterloo.ca/~biedl/), and my Bachelor of Science (Major: Mathematics) at the [Indian Institute of Science (IISc), Bangalore](https://iisc.ac.in/).

My research interests lie at the intersection of algorithms and machine learning.
