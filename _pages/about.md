I am a first-year PhD student in Computer Science at the [University of Wisconsin-Madison](https://www.cs.wisc.edu/), supervised by [Prof. Sandeep Silwal](https://sandeepsilwal.com/) and [Prof. Misha Khodak](https://pages.cs.wisc.edu/~khodak/).

Previously, I completed an MMath in Computer Science at the [University of Waterloo](https://uwaterloo.ca/) under the supervision of [Prof. Therese Biedl](https://cs.uwaterloo.ca/~biedl/), and a BSc in Mathematics at the [Indian Institute of Science, Bangalore](https://iisc.ac.in/).

I am interested in the intersection of algorithms and machine learning.
